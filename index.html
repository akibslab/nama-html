FAQs

How to invest in Nama?
Investing in Nama requires registration and completion of all required information.
How do I know about new opportunities in Nama?
Registered users will be notified of new investment opportunities before they are offered to the public. You can also follow Nama on social media to stay up-to-date on new opportunities.
Is the profit guaranteed in nama?
There is no guarantee of any profit or return, you may lose your money in the investment or you may make a profit according to the returns shown in the opportunity.
How can investment returns be obtained if the investment opportunity is profitable?
The amount of the return on investment is deposited in your own wallet in the Nama application, and the amount can be withdrawn by transferring it to your personal bank account or reinvesting it in a new opportunity as desired.
Can non-Saudis invest in Nama?
Yes, Saudi Arabian residents can invest in Nama if they have a valid Iqama.
